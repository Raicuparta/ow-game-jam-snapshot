Into The Asteroid Belt

You and your friend are in a research mission, where you need to go from the asteroid belt orbits to get close to the black hole you two are studying. But oh no! There are aliens that are trying to stop you! Luckily, if you die you just go back to the start (who knew that weird quantum drive would come in handy in situations like this). Also, you have a set time before the black hole eats everything, so be quick! Enjoy this Pokémon Mystery Dungeon inspired Jam entry. Controls are: WASD/Arrow Keys to move. Shift to make a turn go faster. Note: At the bottom of the screen you have a log of the actions taken by you and the AI until that point.
